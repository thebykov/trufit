Here is how the 4 nutritional cycles are broken down each week.

Day 1 is Revitalize Day.

This day is all about restoring your bodies natural energy stores.

Day 2 is Fat Targeting Day.

This day is all about helping your body burn fat. How are we going to do that? Fat. Yes, believe it or not fat helps you burn fat. However, the right kinds of fat and the proper amounts.

Day 3 is Recharge Day.

This day will add in complex and starchy carbs. Rotating complex and starchy carbs in a 72-hour period does two things. Keeps you from feeling deprived in addition to keeping your metabolism burning and elevated.

Day 4 Cell Rejuvenation Day

These days you will be allowing your body to heal by giving your digestive system a specific scheduled break so it can aid in the healing and recovery of other systems primarily Cell Rejuvenation.
